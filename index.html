Frequently Asked Questions

How will I receive the filled guest data? +

There are 2 options:

1) You receive it via your specified Telegram or WhatsApp

2) You receive applications to your email address

How does payment work? +

Payment is made in 2 stages — 50% in advance, 50% after confirming the final version.

Can I order a custom design? +

Yes, you can send links from Pinterest or Google. Custom design pricing starts from $38. Timeline: 3-5 days.

Can I add music to my invitation? +

Yes! You can add your favorite song or choose from our music library. You can also remove music if you prefer.

Can I update information after the invitation is created? +

Yes, minor changes can be made even after the invitation is completed. Contact us for any updates you need.
