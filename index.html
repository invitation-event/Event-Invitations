Common Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering

How will I receive the guest RSVP responses?

You have two convenient options: 1) Instant notifications sent directly to your Telegram or WhatsApp, or 2) A neatly organized spreadsheet delivered to your email address at the end of the day.

What is the payment structure?

We use a secure 2-stage payment system: 50% advance payment to begin working on your invitation, and the remaining 50% only after you have reviewed and approved the final version.

Can I make changes after the invitation is sent to guests?

Yes! This is the beauty of digital invitations. If the venue changes or you need to update the timing, just let us know, and we will update the live link instantly without needing to resend anything.

Do you provide music integration?

Absolutely. You can provide a link to your favorite song (Spotify, YouTube, or MP3), or choose from our curated library of elegant, royalty-free background music.
