Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How will I receive the guest RSVP data?

You can choose your preferred method: 1) Instant notifications to your Telegram or WhatsApp, or 2) A compiled spreadsheet sent to your email address.

How does the payment process work?

Payment is split into two stages for your convenience: 50% advance payment to start the work, and the remaining 50% after you approve the final version of your invitation.

Can I request a completely custom design?

Absolutely! You can share links from Pinterest, Instagram, or any other source. Our designers will create a unique concept tailored to your vision starting at $38.

Can I change details after the invitation is published?

Yes! One of the biggest advantages of digital invitations is flexibility. Minor text or time changes can be made even after the link has been sent to guests.
